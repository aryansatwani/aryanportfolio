ABOUT

AIM: Decider helps high school students decide on the best college based on their own choices and parameters, saving hours of mechanical work and eventually landing them in their dream universities for free. We aim to assist students globally in this crucial phase of their lives by decreasing their burden and giving them greater time to spend on their admission process in other domains such as Essay writing, tests etc with our free to use and interactive platform.

Who we are? We at Postmen, a student-led community-driven group, have felt the lack of a centralized and free portal that would have tailor-made results for each student, having been in similar situations during our university application phase.
